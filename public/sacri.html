Submission and Camera ready Information

Paper Submission Guideline

  • Prospective authors are invited to submit full papers of 6-8 pages (including tables, figures and references) in standard IEEE double-column format. Our conference focuses on showcasing original research, including completed studies or substantial work in progress.
  • The manuscript template for Microsoft Word can be downloaded from: https://www.ieee.org/conferences/publishing/templates.html
    The manuscript should be Microsoft Word/Latex template (A4 template).

Submission System

Please submit your paper via EDAS New users are required to register with EDAS before paper submission.

Review Process

All submitted papers will undergo a thorough originality check to uphold academic standards. In compliance with IEEE policies, papers with high similarity indices may be disqualified from acceptance. Each submission will be subjected to a rigorous peer-review process conducted by three independent reviewers. The acceptance of a paper will be determined based on the average score and detailed evaluations provided by the reviewers. Authors of accepted papers are required to revise their manuscripts in accordance with the reviewers’ comments and suggestions before final submission for inclusion in the conference proceedings.
